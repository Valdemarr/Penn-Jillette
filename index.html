Penn Jillette

Magician, comedian, author


Penn Jillette is a world famous magician currently on the show "Penn and Teller: Fool Us"


Timeline of Penn's career:

  • 1988 - Jillette was in a speed mariachi power trio called Bongos, Bass and Bob
  • 1990 - Jillette started contributing to the now-defunct PC/Computing magazine, having a regular back-section column
  • 1990 - Jillette was the primary voice announcer for the U.S.-based cable network Comedy Central
  • 1996 - He had a recurring role on Sabrina the Teenage Witch as Drell, the head of the Witches' Council.
  • 1997 - Jillette started writing bi-weekly dispatches for the search engine Excite.com.
  • 2003 - Jillette, along with Teller, began producing and hosting the show Penn & Teller: Bullshit! on Showtime
  • 2005 - With actor Paul Provenza, Jillette co-produced and co-directed The Aristocrats, a documentary film tracing the life of an obscene joke known as "The Aristocrats"
  • 2006 - Jillette started hosting, along with fellow atheist and juggler Michael Goudeau, a live radio talk show broadcast on Free FM
  • 2006 - Jillette hosted the prime-time game show Identity
  • 2010Along with Teller, Jillette began judging on Penn and Teller: Fool Us, a show which they both created
  • 2011 - Jillette's book God, No! Signs You May Already Be An Atheist and Other Magical Tales was released and made the New York Times Best Sellers
  • 2012 - Along with Michael Goudeau, Jillette started the podcast Penn's Sunday School
  • 2013 - Penn and Teller were honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in the live performance category

"One of the things that Teller and I are obsessed with, one of the reasons that we're in magic, is the difference between fantasy and reality."
- Penn Jillette
